Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority. Here's how we protect you:

  • Local Processing: All location data is processed on your device
  • No Data Collection: We don't collect, store, or analyze your location data
  • User Control: You decide when and how to share your location
  • Transparent Permissions: Clear explanations of what permissions we need and why
  • Regular Security Updates: Continuous security improvements and patches
